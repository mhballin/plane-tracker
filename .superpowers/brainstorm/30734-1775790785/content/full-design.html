Status Bar BG

Darkest element — grounds the bottom of the screen
Why these specific darks for a cheap screen: Cheap TFT panels crush dark colors together due to poor gamma. Using 4 distinct dark values (#060e1a → #0a1428 → #0e1726 → #162033) gives visible depth even when the panel compresses them. Pure black #000000 and near-black look identical on many cheap panels, so we avoid it as a background.
